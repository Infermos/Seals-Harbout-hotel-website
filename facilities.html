Hotel Facilities

At Seals Harbour Hotel, we offer a range of facilities designed to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable. From relaxing spaces to exciting activities, we have something for everyone.




Rooms

Our rooms in Weligama offer the perfect blend of coastal comfort and modern luxury. Whether you're here to surf, relax, or explore, each room is designed to be your peaceful retreat—featuring cozy beds, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and private balconies with stunning views of the ocean or lush gardens. With options ranging from budget-friendly doubles to spacious family suites, you'll find the ideal space to unwind after a day in paradise.

Pool

Dive into our refreshing swimming pool or lounge by the poolside with a drink. It's the perfect spot to unwind and soak up the sun.

Rooftop Dine In

Head up to our rooftop for a dining experience with a view. Overlooking the Weligama coastline, it's the perfect place to enjoy delicious local and international dishes while watching the sunset. Our rooftop bar serves up everything from fresh tropical cocktails to fine wines and chilled beers, all in a laid-back, scenic setting.

Surfing

Weligama is one of the best spots in Sri Lanka for beginner-friendly surfing. Just a short walk from the hotel, you'll find plenty of local surf schools offering lessons, board rentals, and guided sessions. The gentle waves and sandy bottom make it perfect for first-timers looking to catch their first wave, as well as more experienced surfers wanting to refine their skills.

Yoga

Reconnect with your mind and body through our guided yoga sessions, offered right here at the hotel. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, our instructors tailor each class to suit all levels. Sessions are held in peaceful, open-air spaces—often at sunrise or sunset—so you can stretch, breathe, and flow with the natural rhythm of Weligama. It's the perfect way to start your day or wind down after a beach adventure.
