At Seals Harbour Hotel, we offer a curated range of facilities designed to make your stay comfortable, exciting, and truly unforgettable. From serene poolside lounging to adrenaline-fuelled surf sessions, we have something for every kind of traveller.

01
Accommodation

Luxury Rooms

Our rooms in Weligama offer the perfect blend of coastal comfort and modern luxury. Whether you're here to surf, relax, or explore, each room is your peaceful retreat — cozy beds, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and private balconies with stunning ocean or garden views.

Explore Rooms
02
Leisure

Infinity Pool

Dive into our refreshing pool or lounge poolside with a fresh tropical cocktail. The perfect spot to soak up the Sri Lankan sun and let the day drift away beneath clear blue skies.

03
Dining

Rooftop Dine In

Head up to our rooftop for a dining experience with a view. Overlooking the Weligama coastline, savour delicious local and international cuisine while watching the golden hour unfold. Our bar serves fresh tropical cocktails, fine wines, and chilled beers in a laid-back, scenic setting.

04
Adventure

Surfing

Weligama is one of Sri Lanka's best beginner-friendly surf spots. Just steps from the hotel, local surf schools offer lessons, board rentals, and guided sessions. Gentle waves and a sandy bottom make it ideal for first-timers and seasoned surfers alike.

05
Wellness

Yoga

Reconnect with your mind and body through guided yoga sessions held right here at the hotel. All levels welcome — our instructors tailor every class. Sessions take place in peaceful open-air spaces, often at sunrise or sunset, in perfect harmony with Weligama's natural rhythm.
